Welcome!

I am associate professor at Department of Statistics at Federal University of Paraná (UFPR, 2010 - today). I lecture Computational Statistics regularly at Bachelor in Statistics and several topics at Data Science & Big Data Specialization. I also teach Elementary Statistics and Applied Statistics, related to the data analysis, frequently.2222222222222222222222222

I am a R programmer and data lover. Because of that, I have experience with R language and I am frequently invited to give short courses related to the subject.
