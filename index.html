Welcome!

This is Yuchen Wang. I am a PhD student in the Urban & Regional Sciences PhD program at Texas A&M University (TAMU). Prior to this, I got my bachelor’s and master’s degrees in urban and rural planning at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), advised by Prof. Helin Liu. In addition, I am good at conducting interdisciplinary research on planning, geography and transportation. My current research interests are centered on Smart Cities and Urban Informatics, specifically focusing on GeoAI, AI planning, Urban Digital Twins, Urban Analytics, Urban Models, Climate-resilient Cities and Cybercrime. (Updated Sep 2025)
