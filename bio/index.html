Welcome!

This is Yuchen Wang (王雨晨). I am a first-year PhD student in the Urban & Regional Sciences PhD program at Texas A&M University (TAMU). Prior to this, I got my bachelor's and master's degrees in urban and rural planning at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). In addition, I am good at conducting interdisciplinary research on planning, geography and transportation. My current research interests are centered on Smart Cities and Urban Informatics, specifically focusing on Urban Digital Twins, Urban Foundation Models, GeoAI, AI for Planning, Urban Analytics, Climate-resilient Cities and Cybercrime.
